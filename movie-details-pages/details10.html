Synopsis

Extraction 3 continues the high-stakes action saga centered on Tyler Rake (Chris Hemsworth), a black-ops mercenary. Following the events of Extraction 2, Tyler and his ally Nik (Golshifteh Farahani) face new challenges after being imprisoned at the end of the second film. Tyler, desperate to reunite with his son, is presented with a dangerous job in exchange for his freedom. Along with his trusted companion Nik, Tyler embarks on another perilous mission that will test his limits and expand the franchise’s intense action sequences​
