Synopsis

This new installment in the Alien franchise, directed by Fede Álvarez, is set on a distant planet, following a group of young individuals who find themselves facing the deadly Xenomorph. The plot explores their survival against the most terrifying life form in the universe, harking back to the tense, claustrophobic atmosphere of the original Alien and Alien 3. Unlike other films in the franchise, it is set between Alien (1979) and Aliens (1986)
