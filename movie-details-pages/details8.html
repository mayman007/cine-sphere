Synopsis

Mufasa: The Lion King is a prequel to Disney's iconic The Lion King. The story follows Mufasa’s early life, from his origins as an orphaned cub to becoming the legendary king of the Pride Lands. Narrated by Rafiki to young Kiara, the film showcases Mufasa's unlikely rise, the bond with his brother Taka (who becomes Scar), and his journey to fulfill his destiny. The film also delves into Mufasa’s formative years and the challenges he faced, highlighting themes of destiny, family, and leadership
